How to play

Are you playing a one or two player game?

  1. For one player:
    • First click "Play against the computer"
    • You will see a 3x3 square grid
    • The game will random assign you a symbol, o or x
    • It will also randomly choose if you or the computer play first
    • Follow the rules below, and see if you can beat the computer!

  2. For two players:
    • Choose a symbol x or o, or decide who will play first
    • Click "Play a friend"
    • You will see a 3x3 square grid
    • The game will randomly choose if x or o plays first
    • Follow the rules below, and enjoy the game!

The rules:

  • The aim is to match three of your symbols, horizontally, vertically or diagonally
  • The first player to match three of their symbols in a row wins
  • Tip: use your symbol to stop your opponent getting three in a row
  • It's really that simple!
